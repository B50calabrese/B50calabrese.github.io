In this tutorial I will be covering the basic concepts behind how an Android launcher is made and how you can customize various parts of the launcher to your own needs. An Android launcher is, in simple terms, the home screen of your Android device. Good examples of various Android launchers include Nova Launcher, Action Launcher 3, and the Google Now Launcher. The appeal to using an alternate launcher, or building your own, is the ability to customize the look and feel of your Android device.

The goal of this tutorial is to gain a better insight on how Android applications are built from the ground up and to have something useful and impressive to show for it at the end. The launcher I will be making is pretty basic, but feel free to divert from some of the things done in the tutorial and make your launcher your own.
